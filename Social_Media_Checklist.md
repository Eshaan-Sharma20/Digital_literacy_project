# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 🔐 General Safety Tips
- Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media.
- Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
- Verify job offers, internships, or messages before responding to avoid scams targeting students.

## 💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Never approve unknown “collect requests” in apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
- Always double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before sending money.
- Do not install unknown apps or screen-sharing apps when instructed by strangers.

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930** for quick assistance in financial fraud cases.
