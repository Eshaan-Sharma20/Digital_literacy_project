# 🔐 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 📋 Actionable Safety Tips for College Students

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
2. Never share OTPs, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
3. Always verify links before clicking, especially those received via SMS, email, or social media.
4. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or accessing sensitive information.
5. Keep your phone, apps, and antivirus software updated regularly.
6. Never approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
7. Remember: you do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
8. Double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before sending any payment.
9. Do not install screen-sharing or unknown apps when instructed by strangers.
10. Be cautious of fake job offers, internships, or messages targeting students.

- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

## 📝 Note
Cybercrime is increasing rapidly with the growth of digital platforms. Most scams exploit human mistakes rather than technical weaknesses. Being alert and informed is the best defense against such threats.

---

## ✅ Conclusion
Following these simple yet effective practices can help students stay safe online. Awareness, caution, and responsible behavior are essential to prevent cybercrime and protect personal and financial information.
